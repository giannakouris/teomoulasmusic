Teo Moulas

About Teo Moulas

Teo Moulas is a distinguished Greek musician, renowned for his versatility as a singer, pianist, and composer. Specializing in the soul-stirring genres of Entexna and Laika, Teo bridges the gap between traditional Hellenic roots and modern global arrangements.

The Heritage of Greek Music

From the ancient lyre to the modern bouzouki, Greek music is the pulse of the Mediterranean. Teo continues this legacy, keeping the flame of Greek culture alive for audiences worldwide through authentic performances that honor history.
