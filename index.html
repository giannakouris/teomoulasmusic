About Teo Moulas

Teo Moulas is a distinguished Greek musician, renowned for his versatility as a singer, pianist, and composer. Specializing in the soul-stirring genres of Entexna and Laika, Teo bridges the gap between traditional Hellenic roots and modern global arrangements. His performances are a journey through the heart of Greece, delivered with technical mastery and deep emotional resonance.

The Heritage of Greek Music

From the ancient lyre to the modern bouzouki, Greek music is the pulse of the Mediterranean. Laika represents the urban folk heart of the people, while Entexna brings orchestral sophistication to poetic lyrics. Teo continues this legacy, keeping the flame of Greek culture alive for audiences worldwide.
